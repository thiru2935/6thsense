About External Systems

External systems integration allows us to securely exchange health data with other healthcare platforms such as hospital EMR systems, labs, and specialist clinics.

Benefits
  • Automatically import lab results
  • Share health readings with your healthcare providers
  • Sync medication information
  • Import clinical notes and visit summaries
Privacy & Security: All data exchanges are secured with encryption and comply with healthcare privacy regulations.
How to Link Systems
  1. Obtain Your External ID - Contact your healthcare provider or check your patient portal to find your patient ID in their system.
  2. Select the System - Choose the correct external system from the dropdown list.
  3. Enter Your ID - Input your external patient ID exactly as provided.
  4. Sync Data - After linking, click the Sync button to begin exchanging data.
