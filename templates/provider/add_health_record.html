Record Type Guidelines

Clinical Records
  • Clinical Note

    Document encounter details, observations, patient complaints, and treatment plans.

  • Progress Note

    Document patient progress, responses to treatment, and adjustments to care plans.

  • Consultation Report

    Document findings and recommendations from a specialist consultation.

  • Discharge Summary

Test Results & Prescriptions

    Include test type, reference ranges, values, and interpretations.

  • Radiology Report

    Document imaging findings, impressions, and recommendations.

  • Prescription

    Include medication name, dosage, frequency, duration, and special instructions.

  • Referral

    Specify referral reason, urgency, and relevant clinical information.
