Test Information

Select a test type to see preparation instructions.

HbA1c Test

Measures your average blood sugar level over the past 2-3 months.

Preparation:
  • No fasting required
  • Continue medications as usual
  • Test takes about 5 minutes
Fasting Plasma Glucose

Measures your blood sugar after an overnight fast.

Preparation:
  • Fast for 8 hours before the test
  • Water is allowed
  • Schedule your appointment in the morning
  • Consult with your doctor about morning medications
Oral Glucose Tolerance Test

Measures how your body processes glucose over time.

Preparation:
  • Fast for 8-12 hours before the test
  • Test takes 2-3 hours to complete
  • You'll drink a glucose solution
  • Multiple blood samples will be taken
  • Bring something to pass the time
