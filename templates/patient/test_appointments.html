About Diabetic Tests

Common Diabetes Tests
  • HbA1c (Glycated Hemoglobin)

    Measures average blood sugar levels over the past 2-3 months

  • Fasting Plasma Glucose

    Measures blood sugar after an overnight fast

  • Oral Glucose Tolerance Test

    Measures how your body processes glucose

Test Preparation Tips
  • Fast for 8-12 hours before fasting glucose tests
  • Check with your doctor about medication adjustments
  • Follow any specific dietary instructions
  • Wear comfortable clothing with easy access to your arms
  • Bring your ID, insurance information, and doctor's referral
